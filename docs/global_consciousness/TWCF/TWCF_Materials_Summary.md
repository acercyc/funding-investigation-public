## Key Information from the Materials

### Global Call for Ideas Overview

**Deadline:** February 13, 2026  
**Contact:** ideas@templetonworldcharity.org

**What They're Looking For:**
- Bold, innovative ideas that could become new funding programs ($5-20M over 5-7 years)
- Ideas that align with TWCF's three core areas:
  1. Fundamental processes of the natural world
  2. Human nature and spiritual progress
  3. Nature of transcendent divinity

**Key Characteristics:**
- **Innovative:** Novel hypotheses, methods, or breakthrough innovations
- **Contrarian:** Ideas that aren't already popular or routinely funded
- **Catalytic:** Ideas that can generate wider interest and attract additional funding
- **Positive:** Advance positive goals rather than negative/pessimistic focus

## Strategic Framing for Your Research

**Your Idea Could Be:**
"Information-Theoretic Approaches to Consciousness: A New Programmatic Direction for Testing Competing Theories"

**Key Points to Emphasize:**
- Adversarial collaboration between competing theories (like TWCF's $20M contest model)
- Bridging neuroscience, AI, and philosophy
- Addressing replication crisis through rigorous methodologies
- Testing theories that public funders find too speculative
- Potential to become a $5-20M program funding multiple research teams
