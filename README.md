## Relevance Scoring System (0-5)

Every funding opportunity is scored for strategic prioritization:

| Score | Category | Description |
|:------|:---------|:------------|
| **5/5** | Perfect Match | Explicitly funds consciousness, "Big Questions," or high-risk interdisciplinary science |
| **4/5** | Strong Match | Core Neuroscience/AI focus; requires framing through clinical or technical lens |
| **3/5** | Moderate Match | General science funding; requires significant alignment effort |
| **2/5** | Peripheral Match | Niche or specific to unrelated sub-fields |
| **1/5** | Low Match | Requires major pivot in research goals |
| **0/5** | No Match | Excluded by eligibility or field |

## Three-Tiered Funding Ecosystem

The project uses a portfolio approach across three geographic levels:

| Tier | Sources | Strategy |
|:-----|:--------|:---------|
| **Finnish** | Kone, Erkko, SKR, Sigrid Juselius, RCF | High-autonomy foundations for "blue-sky" research and initial group building |
| **European** | Horizon Europe, ERC, MSCA | Scale and consortium-based funding for international collaboration |
| **Global** | Templeton, Tiny Blue Dot, FQXi, HFSP | Specialized philanthropy for "Big Questions" and foundational research |
