## EU Funding: Detailed Breakdown

### Pillar I: European Research Council (ERC)

**The most prestigious individual research grants in Europe.**

**Purpose:** Support excellent frontier research by individual researchers. Bottom-up, investigator-driven (no predefined topics).

| Scheme | Target Career Stage | Funding | Duration | Success Rate | Your Eligibility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Starting Grant** | 2-7 years post-PhD | Up to €1.5M | 5 years | ~12-15% | ❌ Too late (PhD 2017 = 8+ yrs) |
| **Consolidator Grant** | 7-12 years post-PhD | Up to €2M | 5 years | ~12-15% | ✅ **Eligible** (Priority #1) |
| **Advanced Grant** | 15+ years post-PhD | Up to €2.5M | 5 years | ~10-12% | ❌ Too early (~2032) |
| **Synergy Grant** | 2-4 PIs (any stage) | Up to €10M | 6 years | ~5-7% | ✅ Possible (2028-2029 target) |
| **ERC Plus Grant** | Any stage (outstanding record) | Up to €7M | 4-7 years | ~1-2% | ✅ Monitor 2026, target 2027-2028 |
| **Proof of Concept** | ERC grantees only | Up to €150k | 18 months | N/A | ❌ Requires existing ERC grant |

#### ERC Consolidator Grant (Your Primary Target)

| Attribute | Detail |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Eligibility Window** | 7-12 years post-PhD (PhD Sep 2017 = **You are eligible**) |
| **2027 Call Opens** | September 25, 2026 |
| **2027 Deadline** | **January 13, 2027** |
| **Funding** | Up to €2 million over 5 years |
| **Success Rate** | ~12-15% |
| **Key Requirement** | Demonstrate scientific independence and track record of significant achievements |
| **Evaluation** | Two-stage (Step 1: Panel review, Step 2: Interviews) |
| **Results** | December 2027 (typically 11 months after deadline) |

**Strategic Advice:**
- Start preparing **now** (6-12 months preparation time)
- From 2027, eligibility window widens to **5-15 years post-PhD** (more flexibility)
- Frame Information Closure Theory as a novel theoretical breakthrough
- Emphasize AI safety, clinical applications, and fundamental understanding of consciousness

#### ERC Synergy Grant (Future Target: 2028-2029)

| Attribute | Detail |
| :--- | :--- |

**Strategic Advice:**

## Funding Model

**Co-Funding Requirement:**

- RCF covers **maximum 70%** of total project costs
- Host institution covers **minimum 30%**

**Eligible Costs:**

- Salaries (researchers, postdocs, doctoral students)
- Direct research costs (materials, equipment, travel)
- Infrastructure and overhead (via institutional overhead coefficients)
- International mobility and collaboration

## University of Helsinki Internal Funding: Detailed Breakdown

**Purpose:** Seed larger applications, fund travel, cover "invisible" costs like Open Access fees, and support early-stage research.

### Travel & Mobility

**Chancellor's Travel Grant (Kanslerin matka-apuraha)**
- **Value:** €800-€1,600
- **Purpose:** Foreign travel for conferences or short research visits
- **Strategy:** Use to fund travel to meet potential Horizon Europe consortium partners

### Proof of Concept & Innovation

**HiLIFE Proof of Concept (HiPOC) Grants**
- **Idea Maturation:** Up to €30,000
- **Invention Maturation:** Up to €50,000
- **Focus:** Life science-related innovations
- **Requirement:** ~20% spend on commercialization (market analysis, IP)
- **Strategy:** Frame "AI Agency" work as diagnostic tool or technological invention

### Open Access Support

**Author Fee Service**
- **Value:** Up to €3,500 per article
- **Limit:** Maximum 6 articles per year
- **Coverage:** Gap fund for Full Open Access journals not covered by library agreements
- **Strategy:** Check library agreements first. This saves thousands of euros in grant budget.

### Doctoral Researcher Funding

**University of Helsinki Research Foundation (Tiedesäätiö)**
- **Value:** Fully salaried doctoral researcher positions (~€150k value)
- **Process:** Indirect application via Doctoral Programs
- **Strategy:** Advocate for top PhD candidates to be nominated by Doctoral Program in Brain & Mind

### Small Grants & Catalyst Grants

**UH Small Grants**
- **Deadline:** January 14 - February 6, 2026
- **Purpose:** Travel and materials

**HSSH Catalyst Grant**
- **Deadline:** September 1 annually
- **Purpose:** Fund interdisciplinary meetings required to build larger Kone or Horizon consortium
